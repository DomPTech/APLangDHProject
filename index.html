Guide To Tennessee Valley Authority

Overview

The Tennessee Valley Authority (TVA) is a federally owned corporation that serves as the nation’s largest public power provider. Founded in 1933 as part of the New Deal, it was designed to modernize the Tennessee Valley through electricity, flood control, and economic development.
